Keeping chestnuts

One needs to leave them in their bristled shells until one wants to
eat them. And with this, leave them in a humid place or the
gutter of some roof.


Scentedcandle from Le Mans

They melt, among the wax & candle, rosin,
that in France is called Burgundian, which is white
& not as brittle & dry as the other rosin. They also
perfume the wick and tip of the wick.


To soften gold

Dissolve caput mortuum in distilled vinegar,
filter & congeal in salt & cement
☉ with this, and it will soften it
wonderfully.


Improve the color of gold

Put aquafortis of vitriol on hot ashes. Cover the
bottle, that is, the mouth, with a tile, then unstop
it & a thick smoke will come out; put the gold on this
and it will take on a high color.
